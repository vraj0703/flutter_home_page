Jotter - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Jotter is designed with privacy at its core. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. Data We Collect

None. Jotter does not collect any personal data. All entries, categories, and settings you create are stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite database.

2. Data Storage

  • Local only: All your data (entries, categories, preferences) is stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
  • No cloud storage: We do not operate any servers or cloud infrastructure that stores your data.
  • No accounts: Jotter does not require you to create an account, sign up, or provide any personal information to use the app.

3. Google Drive Backup (Optional)

Jotter offers an optional Google Drive backup feature. If you choose to enable it:

  • Your database is backed up to your own Google Drive account, in a hidden app-specific folder (appDataFolder).
  • We use the drive.appdata scope, which only allows access to Jotter's own folder — we cannot read or modify any other files in your Google Drive.
  • Backup and restore are initiated by you — nothing happens automatically unless you configure it.
  • Your Google account credentials are handled entirely by Google's authentication system. We never see or store your Google password.

4. Third-Party Services

Jotter does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any data broker or third-party data processor

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to share. Your entries, financial records, health data, and personal notes never leave your device (unless you explicitly back up to your own Google Drive).

6. Children's Privacy

Jotter is a general-purpose utility app. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since the app stores all data locally and collects no information, it is safe for users of all ages.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Delete individual entries from within the app at any time.
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data from your device settings.
  • Delete Google Drive backups by signing into your Google Drive and removing the Jotter backup files from the hidden app data folder.

8. Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security (screen lock, encryption). The local SQLite database is stored in the app's private storage area, which is not accessible to other apps on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
